Items of note — why more datasets matter

universe expands as deals are added

Each new implementation / dataset added to the model does three things:

1Expands the universe

Widens the kitchen-sink Venn — the total universe of represented product capabilities.

2Reveals intensity & value

Highlights different areas of depth, intensity and value across deals.

3Informs roadmap & pricing

Builds data to rank highest-demand products, sub-products, capabilities and features for roadmap prioritization and value-based pricing.
